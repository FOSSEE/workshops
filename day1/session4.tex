\section{Solving linear equations}
\begin{frame}[fragile]
\frametitle{Solution of equations}
Example problem: Consider the set of equations
  \begin{align*}
    3x + 2y - z  & = 1 \\
    2x - 2y + 4z  & = -2 \\
    -x + \frac{1}{2}y -z & = 0
  \end{align*}

  To Solve this, 
  \begin{lstlisting}
    In []: A = array([[3,2,-1],[2,-2,4],[-1, 0.5, -1]])
    In []: b = array([1, -2, 0])
    In []: x = linalg.solve(A, b)
    In []: Ax = dot(A, x)
    In []: allclose(Ax, b)
    Out[]: True
  \end{lstlisting}
\end{frame}
